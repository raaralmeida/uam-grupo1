Urban Air Mobility Research

This project investigates the integration of new entrants such as UAS, UAM vehicles, HAPS and suborbital vehicles into the Air Traffic Management system.

Research Objective

Evaluate the impact of emerging aerial vehicles on ATM performance using Agent Based Modeling.

Research Topics

  • Urban Air Mobility demand forecasting
  • Drone operations analysis
  • Airspace capacity and conflict prediction
  • ATM performance indicators
